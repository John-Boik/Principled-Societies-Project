Donations Are Appreciated

Please consider donating to the Principled Societies Project. Our effort is not yet funded by grants or other major sources and your donation, regardless of size, is appreciated and helpful.

Tax-deductible donations are accepted on our behalf by our fiscal sponsor, We, The World, a US 501(c)(3) nonprofit. Donations can be made on their website, or a check payable to We, The World in U.S. currency can be mailed to the address below. Be sure to mark on the check that the donation is for the Principled Societies Project.

We, The World
P.O. Box 750651
Forest Hills, NY 11375-0651
USA
