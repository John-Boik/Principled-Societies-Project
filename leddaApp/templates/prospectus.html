Social Investor Slide Deck

On this page is a social investor slide deck for Phase I development of the still nascent science-oriented US-based nonprofit corporation tentatively named Socio prospectus. If funded, this organization would carry out the work of developing and testing new social choice systems. The mission of Socio prospectus is to model and forecast collective wellbeing status, starting at the local-area level, and to research, develop, promote, implement, service, and report on new and/or improved social choice system designs.

If you are a foundation or social investor, or have contacts and wish to help, please contact John Boik via the contact page. A confidential social investment prospectus is available on request. An excerpt of the prospectus, containing only background information, is on Medium.com.
